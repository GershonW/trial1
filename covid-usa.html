The USA requires Corona testing for all passengers within 72 hours of the 1st departure flight (assuming a connection under 24 hours.)

This includes people who have had the vaccine or who have tested positive for antibodies

People who have recovered in the last 90 days do not require a negative test

The requirement for a test is for age 2 and up only
